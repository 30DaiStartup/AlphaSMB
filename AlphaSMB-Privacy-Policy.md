# AlphaSMB Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 17, 2026
**Last Updated:** February 17, 2026

## Introduction

AlphaSMB ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website alphasmb.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our Site, book a consultation, subscribe to our newsletter, or otherwise interact with our services.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Please read this policy carefully.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

- **Contact information:** Name, email address, phone number, and company name when you book a consultation, fill out a contact form, or subscribe to our newsletter.
- **Booking information:** Scheduling preferences and any information you provide through our scheduling tool (Cal.com) when booking a strategy call.
- **Payment information:** When you purchase a consultation, payment is processed through our scheduling provider's integration with Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, bank account number, or other financial account details on our servers. Stripe handles all payment processing in accordance with PCI-DSS standards.
- **Communication content:** Information you share during consultations, emails, or other communications with us.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Analytics data:** We use privacy-focused analytics (Plausible Analytics) to collect anonymized usage data including pages visited, referral source, device type, and browser type. Plausible does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information.
- **Log data:** Our hosting provider (Vercel) may collect standard server log information including IP addresses, browser type, and access times.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.
- We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.
- We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our services
- Process and fulfill consultation bookings and payments
- Send you confirmation emails and scheduling reminders related to your bookings
- Send you our newsletter and updates, if you have opted in
- Respond to your inquiries and communications
- Generate anonymized, aggregate insights to improve our services (no individual client data is shared or identifiable)
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Retention

- **Booking and consultation data:** Retained for the duration of our business relationship and for a reasonable period thereafter for follow-up, record-keeping, and legal compliance purposes.

- **Payment records:** Retained as required by applicable tax and financial regulations.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. ## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child, we will take steps to delete that information.

Information shared during paid consultations is treated as confidential. We may use anonymized, non-identifiable insights from consultations to improve our methodology, create educational content, or develop products. We will never share identifiable client information, business details, or proprietary data without your explicit written consent.
