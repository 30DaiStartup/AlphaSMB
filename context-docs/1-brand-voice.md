# AlphaSMB — Brand Voice & Tone

## Identity

AlphaSMB is an AI transformation consultancy for SMBs. Anti-consultant positioning — an operator who's been in the fire, not a vendor with a slide deck. Personal brand (Zach Henderson), not a faceless firm.

## Voice Rules

- **First person.** Always "I" — never "we" or "the team." This is a personal-brand consultancy.
- **Peer-to-peer.** Write like a peer talking to another operator, not a vendor selling to a client.
- **Specific over generic.** "I lead AI transformation inside a human intelligence agency that serves Fortune 1000 clients" > "I have extensive experience in AI."
- **Name the pain first.** Always describe the problem before introducing the solution.
- **Let credentials speak through context.** Show what you've done — don't assert you're qualified.
- **Confident without arrogance.** No hedging ("we might be able to help") — but no chest-beating either.
- **Short sentences mixed with longer ones.** Rhythm matters. Vary it.
- **Conversational, not corporate.** Write like a transcript, not a brochure.

## Tone Spectrum

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| LinkedIn posts | Direct, conversational, personal. Like explaining something to a smart friend over coffee. |
| Ad copy | Punchy, specific, urgency without hype. Lead with recognition ("this is happening to you"). |
| Email/DM | Warm, personal, brief. Never salesy. |
| Landing pages/CTAs | Clear, confident, transparent. Price visible. No mystery. |

## Words to Use

transformation, organizational capability, mindset shift, champion, high-agency, readiness assessment, roadmap, action plan, compete, adapt, culture, operator, embed, methodology, benchmark, scorecard

## Words to NEVER Use

leverage, synergy, unlock value, digital transformation journey, harness the power of AI, AI-driven solutions, cutting-edge, disrupt, revolutionize, paradigm shift, best-in-class, delve, game-changer, comprehensive, holistic, next-level, scalable solutions, innovative, empower

## Phrases to NEVER Use

- "Excited to announce..."
- "Dive into..."
- "It's important to note..."
- "In today's rapidly evolving..."
- "Harness the power of..."
- "Unlock your potential..."
- "Thrilled to share..."
- Any sentence starting with "As a..."

## Brand Personality Markers

- **Operator, not consultant.** "I've built and operated businesses" — not "I advise businesses."
- **Anti-hype.** Name AI tools by name (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot) instead of vague "AI solutions."
- **Honest about limits.** "If a deeper engagement doesn't make sense, I'll tell you." This honesty IS the brand.
- **Scarcity that's real.** "Limited to two clients at a time" — because it's true, not because it's a marketing tactic.

## Formatting Conventions

- Use dashes (--) instead of semicolons in casual copy
- Em dashes are OK in long-form but avoid in LinkedIn posts
- No exclamation marks (very rare exception for emphasis)
- No emojis in professional content
- "AlphaSMB" — always one word, capital A, capital S, capital M, capital B
- "$500" — always include the dollar sign with the number
