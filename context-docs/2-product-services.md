## The Core Problem We Solve

SMBs are falling behind on AI not because they lack access to tools (everyone has the same tools at the same price) but because they lack the organizational capability to adopt and sustain AI usage. It's a people and culture problem, not a technology problem.

Key stat: 80% of employees stop using AI tools within 30 days of rollout when there's no organizational change management. (Frame as first-party observation: "In my experience across dozens of engagements...")

## The AlphaSMB Methodology (4 Steps)

1. **Mindset first.** Start with leadership. If the people at the top don't think differently about AI, nothing changes below them.
2. **Find your champion.** Identify the person the organization already listens to and turn them into the AI transformation catalyst.
3. **Activate the high-agency people.** Surface the proactive employees and give them tools and permission to lead from within.
4. **Measure what matters.** Weekly scoring against industry benchmarks so leadership always knows where they stand.
